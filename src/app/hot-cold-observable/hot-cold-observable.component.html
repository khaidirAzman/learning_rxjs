Hot & Cold Observables

RxJS Observables can either be hot or cold.

In this section, we will have a look at the differences between these two types of Observables. More info can be found here.

By definition, there are 3 main differences between Hot and Cold Observables:

  1. Data production :
    If produced inside the Observable, it is Cold. If produced outside the Observable, it is Hot.
  2. Cast type :
    Cold Observables are unicast. Hot Observables are multicast.
  3. Value emission :
    Cold Observables emit only when subscribed to. Hot Observables emit always even without subscription.

Useful operator to turn Cold Observables to Hot are: shareReplay() and fromEvent()
